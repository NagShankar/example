Lotus Lake - Phase 2

"Lotus Lake" together build on an 18 acres of large space in two phases with Phase-2 in around 14+ acres.

The project started after continuous research for about 6 years we found this location as interesting place to build our first dream project "Lotus Lake" which is going to be developed with high-class amentias to cater to the people working in the neighboring industries.

We are mostly targeting people with white-collar jobs to come and live in our project. If you are not working in those neighboring industrial areas (3 Industrial area within 0.7 km to 3 kms form our project) but yet you want to invest in our project, we guarantee you that your investment will be doubled within 3 years.

Along with Software and Electronic Chip design companies, the industrial area primarily built for Manufacturing, Apparel, Pharmaceutical, Automobile, Infra Development, Biotechnology, Energy, and Natural Power, Bio Solar companies etc.

Though the future lies in Industrial Hub with prime focus on "Make In India" we have other advantages as well.

Other Advantages of the location :

  • Bangalore is just 90 minutes drive whereas Bangalore International Airport is just 45 minutes drive.
  • Government is considering to build cluster towns, to ease the Bengaluru city traffic burden, Both State and Central Government already Inaugurated road widening work SH9 and NH69 respectively on March 28th 2015 and March 31st 2015.
  • Special attention towards to Chikkaballapura district, to host a Greater Bengaluru.
  • After Andhra-Telangana bifurcation, Hindupur which is just a walk away from our project is considered for New district, Smart Town and Bangalore-Chennai Industrial Corridor.
  • Definitely this futuristic place is poised to play vital role for nation building from infra prospective, so we are required to build with world-class design and amenities.
